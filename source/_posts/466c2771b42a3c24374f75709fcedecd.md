### Features
  - Switch Fairies
    - Team 1 (CTRL+Z)
    - Team 2 (CTRL+X)
    - Team 3 (CTRL+C)
    - Team 4 (CTRL+V)
  - Switch Jobs/Classes
    - Job 1 (CTRL+A)
    - Job 2 (CTRL+S)
    - Job 3 (CTRL+D)
  - Zoom in (-) - Zoom out (+)
  - Rotate Camera Using D-PAD Arrows
  - 2 Skill Fairies (E & Q)
  - And More
